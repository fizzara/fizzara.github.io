    Humanities Welcome Week Sticker Design

    For Welcome Week 2022 I was asked to design the stickers that would be given to the incoming first years, as a way to welcome them to the McMaster community!

    To view and download a PDF of my process work, please click here.

    A fun, decorative choice. Reminiscent of the mystery machine from scooby doo, but in bright, summer colours.

    A more subdued choice, similar to old motel signs. Darker colours to fit with other aesthetics, but still bright in its own way.

    A more traditional, circular sticker, like an enamel pin. A retro art look that will match with many other colours, or that would look good by itself.
